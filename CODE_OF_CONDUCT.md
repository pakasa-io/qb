# Code of Conduct

This project expects respectful, professional collaboration.

## Our Standards

Examples of expected behavior:

- act in good faith
- be respectful in technical disagreement
- focus feedback on the code, design, docs, and behavior
- assume other contributors are trying to improve the project
- help keep discussions useful, specific, and constructive

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- discriminatory or demeaning language
- intentionally disruptive behavior
- publishing private information without permission
- bad-faith technical engagement

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, pull
requests, or contributions that violate these standards.

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, contact the repository
maintainers through the repository owner's published contact channel rather than
continuing the discussion in public.
